Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and parsley to taste
Instructions:
  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cooked spaghetti to the pan.
  4. Toss everything together and top with chopped parsley.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar, then beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix in flour and baking soda. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Simple Greek Salad

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt, pepper, and oregano to taste
Instructions:
  1. Combine all vegetables and olives in a bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and seasonings together.
  3. Pour over salad and toss gently.
  4. Top with feta cheese and serve cold.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt and oil
Instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, turmeric, and garam masala for 1 hour.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil.
  3. Add tomato puree and simmer. Add marinated chicken.
  4. Cook until chicken is done, then stir in cream. Simmer and serve with rice.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, poached egg, cherry tomatoes
Instructions:
  1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper
